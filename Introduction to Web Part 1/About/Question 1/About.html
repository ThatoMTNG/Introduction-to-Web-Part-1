Dr Kelly Brown is a general practitioner with a love for families especially loves working with mothers and children.

She obtained her medical degree, MBChB, in 2009 at the University of Pretoria and completed her Diploma in Child Health (DHC) in
2013. She qualified as a South African Lactation Consultant (SALC) with Wits University in 2016.

Dr Brown is practicing from 56 Century City, Cape Town, which is a family and child friendly practice.
